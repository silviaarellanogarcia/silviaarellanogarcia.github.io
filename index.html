About

I am a Machine Learning Master recent graduate from KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Stockholm, Sweden). Previously, I completed a BSc in Electrical Engineering at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (Barcelona, Spain). I have gained research experience in industry (Dolby Laboratories, Electronic Arts) and academia. Currently, I enjoy working on topics related with Deep Learning and Generative AI, with a special interest in the field of animation. I like the idea of working on the intersection of art and technology! Additionally, I am a big fan of animated films, it would be so cool to be able to contribute somehow to their development.

  • Current Location: Stockholm, Sweden

Feel free to contact me through e-mail or LinkedIn if you want to talk about a research topic among my interests or potential opportunities. I would be open to consider relocating if necessary.
