About

I am a recent graduate in Machine Learning from KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Stockholm, Sweden). Previously, I earned a BSc in Electrical Engineering from the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (Barcelona, Spain). I have gained research experience in both industry (Dolby Laboratories, Electronic Arts) and academia.

Currently, I enjoy working on topics related with Deep Learning and Generative AI, with a particular interest in the field of animation. I like the idea of working on the intersection of art and technology! Additionally, I am a big fan of animated films, it would be so cool to be able to contribute in some way to their creation.

Feel free to contact me via email or LinkedIn if you would like to discuss research topics related to my interests or explore potential opportunities. I would be open to consider relocating if necessary.

News

She's In The Game Workshop

March 2025

I participated as a mentor in the She’s in the Game workshop at Electronic Arts, where we guided fourteen teenage girls through the process of creating their first video game. We introduced them to various aspects of game development and showed them that it can be an exciting and rewarding career path!

Challenge Based Innovation @ CERN

Autumn 2022

This project was part of the EU-ATTRACT Academy Program, which aims to explore ways to apply discoveries and breakthroughs from leading European research institutions. We developed a prototype consisting of an electronic device designed to track the precise location of a tumor within a patient’s body, along with software developed in C#. The software reconstructs and visualizes a digital representation of the tumor based on the data received from the device.

BitsxLaMarató

2020, 2021 and 2022 editions

Organized and directed BitsxLaMarató, a hackathon hosted by FIB, BSC, and Hackers@UPC in support of La Marató de TV3, a public Catalan charity initiative that raises funds for biomedical research projects. Around 150 participants spent a weekend developing technological projects that addressed challenges proposed by institutions such as hospitals and research centers. The event also featured outreach talks and activities aimed at raising awareness and sharing knowledge.

HackUPC

Organizer of HackUPC, a student hackathon with 700 participants from around the world. I led the Marketing department, managing communications with hackers and running social media.
